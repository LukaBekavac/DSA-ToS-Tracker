DSA ToS Tracker

Get notified when Very Large Online Platforms update their Terms of Service

How it works

This service monitors Terms of Service documents for Very Large Online Platforms (VLOPs) under the Digital Services Act.

When changes are detected, our system uses AI to summarize the changes and sends you a concise email notification.

This project is built on top of the Digital Services Terms and Conditions Database.
